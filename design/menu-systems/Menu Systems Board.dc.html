JCHAT DESIGN EXPLORATION · MOBILE COMMERCE
20 mobile menu systems.
20 navigation philosophies.
An inspiration library for businesses that sell through an app — restaurants, cafés, bars, food trucks, bakeries, ice-cream shops, hotels, stadiums, cinemas. Each concept changes the information architecture, category model, product layout, cart behavior and gesture language — not just the visual theme.
